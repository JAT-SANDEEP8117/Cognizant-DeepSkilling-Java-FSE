Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an event?

Go to the Registration section, fill in your details, choose an event, and click Register.

How can I search for events?

Use the search box and category filter on the home page.

How do I find nearby events?

Click the "Find Nearby Events" button and allow location access.

What if registration fails?

Check your internet connection and ensure all required fields are filled.
